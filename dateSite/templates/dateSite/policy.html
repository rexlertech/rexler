Privacy Policy

I. Privacy Policy

When you visit any website, you are providing a range of personal information to the owners of the website about your visit, depending on what actions you take. This is also the case on Meetlove.com. You should always understand the privacy policies on any website when supplying information during your use of the website. You should always ask if the site does not make the necessary information available to you.

We will only use the information you send to us during your visit for the purposes of internal tracking. This includes the activities of making Meetlove.com effective for you to use and making appropriate responses to your requests for information and other features or services. Unless specifically mentioned, we do not use your personal information for any other reason. We will never sell your information to a third party, and we will never give a third party access to your information.

II. Notice

When you visit Meetlove.com, we make a record of the Internet provider’s domain name and country that you visited from (e.g., ‘aol.com’) and the course you take as you navigate from one page to another (i.e., “click stream” activity). Information requests and visitor registration When you use or register on the Meetlove.com website, we only collect personal information that you provide of your own accord. “Personal information” includes that which can identify you as a certain individual, such as your name or email. For those making affiliate agreements, this information may also include your name and company name. It is possible to browse our site without registering but you will need to register for the purpose of using certain features that we offer. Use of cookies and browsing data We use cookie technology on Meetlove.com as part of the statistical reporting on our website. A cookie is a small piece of information delivered by a website that is saved to your hard disk by the browser on your computer. It contains information used by a website to make your experience more personal (e.g. rotating profiles on the Meetlove.com home page) and to gather statistical data, such as which pages are visited, what is downloaded during your experience, the domain name and country of the internet provider that you have come from (e.g. ‘aol.com’, or ‘umich.edu’), and addresses of those websites you have visited just before, and after, coming to Meetlove.com. Nevertheless, we do not associate any of this information with you as an individual. Only an aggregate measure is taken. Cookie information allows us to follow your “click stream” activity (i.e., how visitors navigate our site as they move from page to page). It is important to note that cookies never capture your individual email address or any of your private information.

We also operate standard Web server log files to assist in counting visitor numbers and analyzing our website’s technical capability. This information is used to establish how many people visit Meetlove.com, enable us to organize the pages for the best usability, make the website suitable for your browser, and ensure that our pages are working at their full potential. Although we collect information on site traffic we do not record information on particular visitors to our site, which means that no information about you as an individual is retained or used.You are able to browse the website without cookies by configuring your browser to reject all cookies, or to give you warning when a cookie is set. However, because every browser is different you should refer to the “Help” menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences. Information you provide and how we use it The information you provide to us is used in several ways:

  • Organize the website for the best possible usability
  • Satisfy your requests for features and services
  • Tailor your website browsing experience
  • Notify you about special offers and other features (if you wish to get such notices)
  • Manage your account and preferences
  • Analyze your use of the website, features and services to make improvements
  • Detect and protect against fraudulent transactions and other suspicious website activity
  • Resolve your questions or enquiries

III. Choice

You can decide which correspondence to receive from us in accordance with our policy on freedom of choice and opting out. Meetlove.com may regularly send emails to you that give information on updates, new features, and special offers connected with our website. You can decide to stop getting these emails whenever you wish. To stop receiving these emails you can click the unsubscribe link found at the bottom of each email you receive or alternatively email us at unsubscribe@Meetlove.com.

IV. Access to your details

If you have a member account on our website, you can use the following instructions to change the use of your personal information.

To change or delete your personal information, please carry out these instructions:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Click the profile thumbnail at the top right hand corner of the Meetlove.com main page
  3. Select My Profile
  4. Edit your personal information
  5. Click Save.

V. Security

We are dedicated to protecting the personal information we collect from you and ensure all security measures are taken to make this happen. Meetlove.com operates firewalls and has implemented all necessary security measures at its physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse, or modification of information that you have provided to us by using our website.

Meetlove.com has secure certificates from VeriSign and McAfee that show the website is approved, fully tested, and certified by these two expert security systems. This means that you are safe from any viruses, identity theft, spyware, fraudulent credit card activity, spam, and internet scams on our website.

VI. Customer service and recourse

Please be advised that whenever you send any information to us relating to any Meetlove.com services, you agree to Meetlove.com’s processing of this information in the United States/Russia in order to provide the services you are using.

Please contact our Customer Service team at support@Meetlove.com if you have any enquiries, comments or issues about Meetlove.com’s Privacy Policy and/or how it relates to you.

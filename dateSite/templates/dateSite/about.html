About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1993 by a Russian-American couple who met by chance, Meetlove is an innovative dating site built on the belief that love knows no boundaries.

Our History

Meetlove was one of the first companies to develop a platform designed to connect people from around the world and it remains at the forefront of its field, continually developing improved communication technologies that allow people to discover and enjoy the possibilities of an exciting, globally-interconnected future. The site now has over 20 million members, more than 80 million visitors annually and 1.5 million+ messages exchanged daily.

Our Team

Meetlove is proud to have an international team working in USA, Columbia, Russia, China, and Marocco with over 300 full-time employees. Meetlove’s long-standing reputation is a testament to the company’s ongoing pursuit of innovation, sustained dedication to member satisfaction, and unwavering commitment to its core values.

Our Mission

Meetlove continually strives to develop innovative communication technologies that inspire global connections and facilitate modern love in the modern world.
